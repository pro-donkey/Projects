Classic Homemade Pizza Recipe



    Ingredients:
  • For the Pizza Dough:
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F/43째C)
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • For the Pizza Sauce:
  • 1 (14-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • For Toppings (Customize as Desired):
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced pepperoni
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced black olives
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Sliced red onion
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
    Instructions:
  • 1. Prepare the Pizza Dough:
  • In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  • Stir until a dough forms, then knead it on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until it has doubled in size.
  • 2. Prepare the Pizza Sauce:
  • In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the crushed tomatoes, minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  • Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  • 3. Preheat the Oven:
  • Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven as it preheats.
  • 4. Shape the Pizza:
  • Punch down the risen pizza dough and divide it into two equal portions for two 12-inch pizzas or make one large pizza if desired.
  • On a floured surface, roll out the dough into a circle or rectangle, depending on your pizza pan or stone.
  • 5. Assemble the Pizza:
  • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  • Spread a generous amount of pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your favorite toppings.
  • 6. Bake the Pizza:
  • Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes (or longer for a larger pizza) until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  • 7. Serve:
  • Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a minute, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and slice into wedges or squares.
  • Enjoy your classic homemade pizza!
