Classic Lasagna Recipe



    Ingredients:
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450 grams) ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6-ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5-ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    Instructions:
  • 1. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • 2. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
  • In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  • Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until the onion is translucent.
  • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, salt, and black pepper. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • 3. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture:
  • In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, eggs, and chopped fresh parsley. Mix well until smooth and well combined.
  • 4. Assemble the Lasagna:
  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce to prevent sticking.
  • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  • Spread half of the ricotta mixture evenly over the noodles.
  • Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese over the ricotta layer.
  • Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, remaining ricotta mixture, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Top with a final layer of meat sauce, remaining mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  • 5. Bake:
  • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly, and the cheese is golden brown.
  • 6. Serve:
  • Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with additional fresh parsley, if desired.
  • Enjoy your classic Lasagna with a side of garlic bread or a simple green salad!
