Diseases of Soyabean



    Soybean Mosaic Virus
  • use high-quality, virus-free seed.
  • liminate virus-infected plants in the field by rouging.
  • use SMV resistant varieties.
  • plant pathogen-free seed.
  • rotate soybean with crops that are not susceptible to the pathogen, such as maize and sorghum.
  • after harvest, bury plant residue completely by plowing the field clean.
  • do not cultivate when foliage is wet
