Classic Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) Recipe



    Ingredients:
  • For the Marinade:
  • 1 pound (450 grams) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste (made from equal parts minced ginger and garlic)
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Salt, to taste
  • For the Sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 (14-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and sugar, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped, for garnish
  • For Cooking:
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)
  • For the Sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 (14-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and sugar, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped, for garnish
  • For Cooking:
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)
    Instructions:
  • 1. Marinate the Chicken:
  • In a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, ground turmeric, ground cumin, ground coriander, chili powder, and salt.
  • Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or ideally, marinate overnight for maximum flavor.
  • 2. Cook the Marinated Chicken:
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil or ghee in a skillet or pan over medium-high heat.
  • Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Set aside.
  • 3. Prepare the Sauce:
  • In the same skillet, add 2 more tablespoons of vegetable oil or ghee if needed.
  • Add the finely chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent and lightly browned.
  • Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  • Add the ground cumin, ground coriander, chili powder, and garam masala. Sauté for 2 minutes, stirring constantly to avoid burning the spices.
  • Pour in the crushed tomatoes, heavy cream, and unsalted butter. Stir well to combine.
  • Season the sauce with salt and a pinch of sugar to balance the flavors. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens and the oil starts to separate from it.
  • 4. Combine the Chicken and Sauce:
  • Return the cooked chicken pieces to the skillet with the sauce. Stir to coat the chicken with the creamy tomato sauce.
  • Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • 5. Serve:
  • Transfer the Butter Chicken to a serving dish.
  • Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro leaves.
  • Serve your classic Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) hot with naan, rice, or your favorite Indian bread.
