Classic Dosa Recipe



    Ingredients:
  • For the Dosa Batter:
  • 2 cups parboiled rice (or regular rice)
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Water, as needed
  • Salt, to taste
  • For Making Dosas:
  • Vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)
    Instructions:
  • 1. Prepare the Dosa Batter:
  • Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds thoroughly. Soak them together in enough water for about 4-6 hours or overnight.
  • Drain the soaked ingredients and grind them to a smooth batter using a blender or wet grinder. Add water as needed to achieve a medium-thick consistency similar to pancake batter.
  • Add salt to the batter and mix well. Allow the batter to ferment by covering it with a lid. Place it in a warm place for approximately 8-12 hours or overnight. The batter should double in volume and develop a slightly sour aroma.
  • 2. Prepare the Dosa:
  • Heat a non-stick skillet or dosa pan over medium-high heat. Grease it lightly with a small amount of vegetable oil or ghee.
  • Pour a ladleful of the dosa batter onto the center of the hot pan. Quickly spread it outward in a circular motion to create a thin, even layer. You can make the dosa as thin or thick as you prefer.
  • Drizzle a few drops of oil or ghee around the edges of the dosa and in the center.
  • Cook the dosa for 2-3 minutes until the edges start to lift and turn golden brown. Use a spatula to gently lift and flip the dosa to cook the other side for an additional 1-2 minutes until it's lightly browned.
  • Repeat the process for the remaining batter, greasing the pan lightly before each dosa.
  • 3. Serve:
  • Transfer the dosas to a serving plate. Serve hot with your choice of chutney (coconut, tomato, or mint), sambar, or potato masala.
  • Enjoy your classic dosa, a popular South Indian breakfast dish, and a versatile meal for any time of the day!
