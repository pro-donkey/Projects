Classic Burger Recipe



    Ingredients:
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles
    Instructions:
  • 1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  • 2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Season both sides of each patty with salt and pepper.
  • 3. Place the burger patties on the grill or pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time to your desired level of doneness.
  • 4. While the burgers are cooking, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  • 5. Once the burgers are done, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a minute.
  • 6. Assemble the burgers by placing a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each bun, followed by a burger patty, sliced tomatoes, sliced onions, and pickles.
  • 7. Top with the other half of the bun and serve your delicious classic burgers with your favorite condiments and side dishes.
