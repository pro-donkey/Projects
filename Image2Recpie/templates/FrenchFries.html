Classic French Fries Recipe



    Ingredients:
  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Salt, to taste
    Instructions:
  • 1. Prepare the Potatoes:
  • Peel the potatoes and cut them into even-sized, rectangular sticks, about 1/4 to 1/2 inch wide. Rinse them in cold water to remove excess starch.
  • Place the cut potatoes in a large bowl of cold water and let them soak for about 30 minutes. This helps remove more starch and makes the fries crispy.
  • After soaking, drain the potatoes and pat them dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  • 2. Preheat the Oil:
  • Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large, heavy-bottomed pot to 325째F (163째C).
  • You should have enough oil to fully submerge the fries.
  • 3. First Fry:
  • Carefully add a small batch of the potato sticks to the hot oil using a spider strainer or slotted spoon.
  • Fry them for about 4-5 minutes until they are pale golden but not yet crispy.
  • Remove the partially cooked fries from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined tray or plate.
  • Repeat this process with the remaining batches of potatoes.
  • 4. Heat the Oil for the Second Fry:
  • Increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C).
  • 5. Second Fry:
  • Return the partially cooked fries to the hot oil in small batches.
  • Fry them for an additional 2-3 minutes until they are golden brown, crispy, and delicious.
  • Remove the fries from the oil and place them on fresh paper towels to drain any excess oil.
  • 6. Season and Serve:
  • While the fries are still hot, season them with salt to taste.
  • Transfer them to a serving platter and enjoy your classic French fries!
  • 7. Optional: Serve with Ketchup or Dipping Sauces:
  • French fries are often served with ketchup, mayonnaise, aioli, or other favorite dipping sauces. Feel free to enjoy them with your preferred condiment.
